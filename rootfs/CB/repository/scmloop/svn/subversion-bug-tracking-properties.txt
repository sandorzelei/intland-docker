The subversion properties as below must be configured to use subclipce and/or tortoise with CodeBeamer.
The content of "bugtraq:url" depends on your Codebeamer installation.

bugtraq:append			false
bugtraq:message			#%BUGID%
bugtraq:number			true
bugtraq:url				https://codebeamer.com/cb/issue/%BUGID%
bugtraq:warnifnoissue	true
